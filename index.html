BioMinecraft?

Minecraft provides a handy way of creating 3D worlds. It also provides an interface that's recognised and understood by many millions of people. This makes it a pretty useful tool to explain aspects of Molecular Biology (looking at the molecular machinery of the cell).

Background

A while ago I figured it might be fun to make Minecraft worlds out of PDB structures: partly to try and explain what I do at work to my kids, partly because it seemed like an interesting challenge that no one else had done.

There's always been a lot of interest whenever I've mentioned this to people in my field (well certainly the people with young kids), so I finally got round to making a video and sticking it up on this web site.

My intention is to add scripts, libraries and instructions to help other people import PDB structures into their own Minecraft worlds and start playing - bear with me, I have a day job.
